Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and appointments

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us, filling out our contact form, or visiting our clinic in person. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, any relevant medical history, and a list of current medications. For hair treatments, it's helpful to bring photos of your desired look.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes, we offer free consultations for all new clients. During the consultation, we'll discuss your beauty goals and recommend the best treatments for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for appointment cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee. Please call us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Do you accept insurance?

Most of our services are cosmetic and not covered by insurance. However, some medical procedures may be covered. Please contact your insurance provider for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments. We also offer financing options for larger treatment packages.
